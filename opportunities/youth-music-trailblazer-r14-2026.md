## Notes

This is the highest-scoring opportunity in the pipeline. Youth Music's entire mission overlaps with WFBA's outreach work. Key fit signals:
- Focus on young people facing barriers (access, cost, geography)
- Safeguarding as core requirement — WFBA has DBS and safeguarding framework in place [inferred: public website]
