## Notable findings

### RBWM Grants — charity registration not required
The Royal Borough of Windsor and Maidenhead grants scheme requires "not-for-profit voluntary organisations with a constitution/terms of reference." This does NOT require registered charity status. This is a significant change from previous assessment. WFBA may be eligible to apply for the September 2026 round if delivery activity in the RBWM area can be confirmed. Deadline historically 30 September.

### Youth Music Trailblazer — charity registration confirmed not required
Youth Music confirms "constituted UK-based organisations" can apply for the Trailblazer Fund. This removes the ambiguity about charity registration. Round 14 (Oct–Nov 2026) is WFBA's strongest medium-term prospect. Begin project brief preparation in August 2026.

### Surrey Community Music Fund — explicitly confirmed as sub-fund
Surrey Community Foundation's main grants round explicitly includes the **Surrey Community Music Fund**, described as supporting "music projects which enable young people to benefit from regular music activities, who would not otherwise have the opportunity to participate." This is an excellent thematic match. EOI opens 25 May 2026; deadline 2 July 2026. Draft EOI using brass education access framing.

### Surrey CC Your Councillor Fund — inter-cycle closure (not permanent)
The website shows "closed to new applications" — this is the normal inter-cycle gap, not a permanent closure. Based on the annual pattern (opened May 6 2025, opened May 2024), the 2026-27 cycle should open around May 5-10 2026. Email Cllr Marisa Heath at marisa.heath@surreycc.gov.uk within the next 2 days.

### Henry Smith Charity — Surrey permanently closed
The Henry Smith Charity's Surrey County Grants programme has permanently closed. It will not reopen. Remove from any future search scope.

### Reaching Communities — new medium-term prospect added
National Lottery Reaching Communities (rolling, £20k–£500k) has been added as a `monitor` opportunity. No charity registration required. Strong missions match but minimum grant size and evidence requirements make this a 2027+ target. Begin building toward it by collecting outreach delivery data.
